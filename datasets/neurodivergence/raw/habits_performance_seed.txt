For many autistic people, a "habit" only sticks when it is paired with a predictable sensory or interest anchor and does not require decision-making in the moment. Pre-commit the entire sequence the night before.

Sustainable output for ADHD brains often comes from "interest-based" rewards delivered immediately after micro-completions rather than large delayed goals. Gamify the 5-minute start.

Pattern: energy accounting. Track "spoons" or "tokens" across physical, cognitive, emotional, sensory, and social domains daily. Design weeks that do not chronically overdraft any domain.

Optimizing human capability: leverage monotropism (deep single focus) by protecting long blocks for deep work and scheduling recovery and transition time explicitly. The "wasted" transition time is actually protective.

Lifestyle: consistent sleep timing + morning light + movement that is rhythmic and predictable (walking, rocking) supports regulation better than high-variability "optimal" exercise for many neurodivergent profiles.
